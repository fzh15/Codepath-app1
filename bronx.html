MTA Officially Licensed NYC Subway Sign - Uptown & The Bronx 4-5-6

This sign is approved by the MTA (Metropolitan Transit Authority) and meets their quality standards as an authentic reproduction sign. It is based on New York City's subway signs.


ABOUT THIS SIGN
Each sign is handmade one at a time from MDF wood and painted flat black. All circles and text are screen printed by hand. I don't use any vinyl, cut out, or stick on letters.

PLEASE NOTE
This listing is for Uptown & The Bronx 4-5-6 and product photos showing other signs are just to show details of a finished sign.
